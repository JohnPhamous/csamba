The Window to the Brain - Undergraduate Research Club

The Window to the Brain - Undergraduate Research Club at UC Riverside was established to provide a community for undergraduates who are working on or want to work on research. We strive to improve the undergraduate research environment here on campus, while also promoting the STEM field in the overall broader community outside of UC Riverside through outreach.
